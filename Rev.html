rEvolution a Spirtiual Movement

Increase your access to God and ultimately your spiritual power by using web and mobile based solutions for tracking spiritual growth.

Think about your access to God as a simple electric circuit... a battery connected by wires to a light bulb.

There are two ways to make your light bulb glow brighter, (1) use a stronger battery and (2) use a higher gauge wire

rEv uses this basic principal to increase Spiritual Power, make your spiritual light bulb glow brighter. rEv looks to increase (1) Spiritual Potential (battery) and (2) Spiritual Current (wire).

Spiritual Potential

Use rEv to increase Spiritual Potential through three major methods, Spiritual Knowledge, Praise & Worship, and Prayer & Meditation. We track the impact of various forms of spiritual media on our users and make suggestions based on past history and similarities between other users. Members are also able to make suggestions to others and view the impact of specific types of media as a function of time. The following are the current types of media that rEv tracks to increase your potential:

  • Spiritual Knowledge
    • Books
    • Religious Texts
    • Articles
    • Videos
  • Praise & Worship
    • Songs
    • Albums
    • Videos
  • Prayer & Meditation
    • Videos
    • Articles
    • Art

Spiritual Current

Use rEv to increase Spiritual Current by monitoring the route that you take on your individual spiritual journey. Members are able to journal about things that have impacted their spirit in day-to-day life. Those entries are able to be shared and the impact rated. The following are the current groupings of journal entries we track:

  • Obedience
  • Sensitivity
  • Humility
  • Faith
  • Other

Spiritual Power

Use rEv to track the resulting increase in Spiritual Power. Track your spiritual growth through your attitudes and actions. Users are able to endorse other users attitudes. They can also track their actions. How are you Doing Good? How much are you Giving? The following are the current Spiritual Power groupings:

  • Attitudes & Endorsements
    • Top Attitudes
    • Other Attitudes
  • Actions

    • Giving
